Knitting

Knitting is using 2 needles on yarn. This is used often to create clothes, beanies and more.

When using needles there could be many sizes. You could see more here.

Needle Type

Knitting has many needles to use. Depending on the project there could be a lot of tools to help a person along the way of creating something with yarn. There are many types, but the main needles known to people who knit are straight needles (for flat projects), circular needles (for large flat pieces and working in the round), and double-pointed needles (DPNs) (for small circumferences in the round, like socks or sleeves).

Knitting Tips

  • Making a neater cast on. To create a better cast on so that project doesn't mess, we cast on using two needles instead of one. Once you have your desired cast one numbered, remove one of the needles and continuing knitting or perling. Having stretched stitches will improve the begining well.

  • When having a dropped stitch on a knitting project. No need to worry. A simple fix is to grab a hook and "crochet" a little to bring back to the original stitch.

      This is the best way to fix a dropped stitch in knitting.
